Site Purpose

The main purpose of the highpeakchamber.com website will be to find creative partnerships of businesses and professionals working together to build a healthy economy and improve the quality of life in a community. We will achieve this by connecting professionals with businesses displaying contact lists, sharing a calendar of activities, a for-hire panel, and more.
